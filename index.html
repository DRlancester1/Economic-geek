About Me

Hello! I’m Dr. Adrian Lancaster – a self-proclaimed economic geek and passionate writer with over 20 years of experience in the world of finance and academia. I earned my Ph.D. in Economics from Ukrainian Catholic University and have since dedicated my career to unraveling the complexities of global markets and economic policies.

I founded Economic Geek in 2020 to share my personal insights, research, and reflections on economic trends, inflation, and market dynamics. My goal is to make economic theory accessible and engaging for everyone—from budding economists to curious readers eager to understand how financial forces shape our lives.

When I'm not immersed in data or scribbling down my thoughts, you’ll find me exploring innovative financial technologies or enjoying a quiet afternoon at a local café, pondering the next big economic trend.
